Here are the keys for most shaders:

  • [ `, 1-9 ]: Set pixel resolution

  • - and +: Raise or lower gameplay speed

  • 0: reset gameplay speed

    • Here are the keys for sonic starships:

  • SPACE: Continuous star

  • H: Full screen

  • V: Verbose

  • I: Increase zoom In

  • O: Increase zoom Out

  • P: Set zoom threshold to average for next second

    • Here are the keys for Tree of Life:

    (use this link for all of these hotkeys on mobile, though it may not work)

  • [Q-U]: Tree of Life colorings

  • D: Sprouting Tree of Life

  • X: X fractal

  • B: 12 petals

  • A: Mandelbrot

  • G: Manny zoom

  • N: Toggle popping clovers!

  • L: Set zoom to freerange

  • C: keep focus on Center

  • J: Carousel

  • ;: Meta-Carousel

  • F: Four Creatures\Bride in zoomer

  • Z: Four Creatures on top

  • M: Four Creatures wheel flip

  • > and <: Raise or lower metronome rate

  • S: Engage morphing! This also works with the Mandelbrot.
