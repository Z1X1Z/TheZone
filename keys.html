Here are the keys for most shaders:

  • [`,1-9,0]: set pixel resolution

  • - and +: raise or lower resolution a little

    • Here are the keys for sonic starships

  • SPACE: Continuous star

  • Z: Pointed\Rectangular Star

  • I: Increase zoom In

  • O: Increase zoom Out

    • Here are the keys for Tree of Life:

  • [Q-U]: Tree of Life color modes

  • A: Mandelbrot

  • F: Four Creatures\Bride

  • X: Seed Sprouting

  • > and <: raise or lower metronome rate
